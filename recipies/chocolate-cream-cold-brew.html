Chocolate Cream Cold Brew

Delicious and refreshing summer drink

Perfect for a hot summer day, ready in under 5 minutes!

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons heavy whipping cream
  • 1 tablespoon milk
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons chocolate syrup
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons cocoa powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup cold brew coffee

Instructions

  1. Add heavy whipping cream, milk, chocolate syrup, cocoa powder and vanilla into a tall glass.
  2. Whip with a milk frother until until desired consistency is reached.
  3. Serve over cold brew coffee.
